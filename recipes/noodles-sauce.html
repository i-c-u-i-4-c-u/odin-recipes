Noodles Sauce

Description

Noodles sauce, also known as pasta sauce or pasta topping, refers to a sauce or condiment that is typically served with cooked noodles or pasta. It is used to enhance the flavor, texture, and overall enjoyment of the dish.

Ingredient

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Marinara sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • Salt for boiling water
  • Optional: grated Parmesan cheese, fresh herbs for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti noodles and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat the marinara sauce in a separate saucepan over medium heat until warmed through.
  3. Once the spaghetti noodles are cooked, drain them in a colander.
  4. Return the drained spaghetti to the pot or transfer it to a serving dish.
  5. Pour the desired amount of marinara sauce over the cooked spaghetti, tossing well to coat the noodles evenly.
  6. Serve the spaghetti with marinara sauce hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs if desired.
